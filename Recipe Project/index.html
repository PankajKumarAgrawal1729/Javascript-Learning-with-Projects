Sahi Panner

Ingredients

  • 300gm paneer
  • 2 tbsp cashewnut paste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup onions, pureed
  • 1 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 1/2 tbsp paneer masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp kasuri methi powder
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream

Recipe Instruction

  1. Prepare Onion Puree
  2. Prepare Tomato Puree
  3. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste
  4. Cook Tomato Puree
  5. Add Yogurt
  6. Add Paneer Masala
  7. Add Cashewnut Paste
  8. Add Cottage Cheese and Spices

Sahi Veg-Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup full-fat plain yogurt
  • Cooking oil, for the bowl
  • 12 ounces paneer, crumbled
  • 1 stick melted unsalted butter
  • Kosher salt

Recipe Instruction

  1. Prepare the Yeast Mixture
  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients
  3. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
  4. Knead the Dough
  5. Let the Dough Rise
  6. Prepare the Dough for Cooking
  7. Cook the Naan on the Stovetop
  8. Broil the Naan with Cheese
